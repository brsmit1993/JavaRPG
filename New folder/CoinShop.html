The Coin Shop

-There are two different locations that when you enter them on the map you will be prompted with the
-option to buy items from said shop. Make sure you have enough space in your inentory though.
-If you buy an item and choose not to drop something else from your inventory you'll be out
-the gold and the item you bought. You can also mug the shop keeper for a small reward.
-But if you aren't strong or lucky enough you'll fail and take damage.
