Events

-There are two different types of events.
-The first is a random chance event. These events do not allow you to make a decision and sort them selves out.
-The second is a choice event. These events allow you to make one of three decisions.
-Both events have 3 possible different outcomes. Generally speaking most events have one bad, one good, and
-one neutral outcome. How ever in some cases a good out come might become bad if you fail the stat check.
-The stat check essentially says if your certain stat isn't high enough then the event becomes bad.
-Some events reward XP, others increase a random stat, and some give random items.
-If the out come is bad though then you'll take damage or lose a stat instead.
-The neutral outcome does nothing.
