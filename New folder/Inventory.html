Iventory and Items

-Through out the game you will be rewarded with a variety of different items.
-Items like potions are known as consumable. These potions will heal or boost your stats.
-Consumeables when used will dissapear from your inventory. While non consumeables will not.
-Non consumeables will give you certain benefits and help you through out the game.
-Make sure to use your space wisely. You only get 10 spots to store items.
-If you get an item while your inventory is full you'll be promted to drop an
-item but you don't have to.
