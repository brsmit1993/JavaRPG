Monster Battles

-At the beginning of the battle who ever has the highest speed attacks first.
-After that it's 100% turn based.
-You will be give 5 options during battle.
-The first is your basic attack. The damage for this is modified by strength.
-Next will be your option to flee. If you attempt to flee but fail you will lose your turn.
-If you are successful then you the battle will end and it will be the next players turn.
-After Flee is your 3 abilities. These are affected by your classes Main Stat
-Damage is also affected by the attackers luck as well as the defense of the person being attacked.
-When one of the two fighters dies the battle will end.
-If the opponenet is a monster you will also be rewarded with XP as well as a random item.
